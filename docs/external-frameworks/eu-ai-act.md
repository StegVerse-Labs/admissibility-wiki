## Definition

The EU AI Act is Regulation (EU) 2024/1689, a legal framework for artificial intelligence in the European Union.

## Relationship To Admissibility

The EU AI Act is useful as a regulatory crosswalk target for obligations, prohibited practices, high-risk systems, transparency, governance, and documentation.
