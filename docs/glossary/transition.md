# Transition

A transition is a movement from one governed state to another.

In StegVerse vocabulary, a transition is not merely a technical change. It is a governance-relevant change that may carry consequence.

## Transition Components

A transition can include:

- an originating state;
- a proposed target state;
- an actor or agent;
- an authority claim;
- a policy reference;
- an evidence posture;
- a review posture;
- a decision result;
- a receipt.

## Transition Question

The core transition question is:

> May this proposed state change bind under the current authority, policy, evidence, and context conditions?
