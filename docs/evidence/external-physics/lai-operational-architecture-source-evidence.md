The accompanying post described the paper as neither a completed theory nor a proof. It presented the work as a map of the problem and suggested that General Relativity and quantum mechanics may represent different descriptive layers of an underlying system.

The paper repeatedly separated demonstrated, sketched, conceptual, speculative, and open claims.
